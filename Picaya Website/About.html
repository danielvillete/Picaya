About us

L.A. Chara Co. is a dynamic manufacturing business dedicated to creating exceptional food products using papaya as our main ingredient. Established in 2018, L.A. Chara Co. is a partnership type of business owned by Ms. Michaela Lorraine Lupian and Kim Aubrey Cuizon, two college friends with a shared passion for entrepreneurship and a deep understanding in business management.











MISSION

L.A. Chara Co. shall provide pickled delicacies suited to Asian palates while at the same time retaining Filipino identity. Through our meticulous ingredient selection and dedication to traditional recipes, we capture the authentic flavors of Filipino cuisine while also embracing the diverse culinary traditions and preferences of Asian cultures.

VISSION

L.A. Chara Co. is a Philippine-based company recognized as the leading manufacturer of pickled products while providing livelihood to papaya farmers in the province of Cavite, Philippines.
These initiatives promote stronger relationships with local communities and positively impact their well-being.
